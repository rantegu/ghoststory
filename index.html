It was a dark, starless night. "The house was a mistake", you said, gazing over the mountains, the sea, the countless trees rising from the earth like a living end, as a desperate, last breath before dawn. “The sea was a mistake. The view was terrible. My hands, my poor hands...dirty, as soon as you touch them. My body, arid, dry. The city, our last steps, a mistake. The last day we’ve been together. The promises. A mistake“. "Come ti pare", I replied. But the words spoke the truth, shiny as the moon that enlightened our faces, shiny as a clean-cut blade.

"I dreamt of you. You were a small child with a pure mind. You spoke with a small, thin voice, as quiet as a small village welcomed by the first light of the day. But you spoke as you knew the secret of nature, you spoke the language of gods. You said: rivers, silence. And everything went dark".

You closed your eyes. This is the end, you said. It’s up to you, I replied, mumbling. A car of young people came by. They were shouting, having fun, kissing. It was the sign that there was a world full of love, out there, waiting for us. But we knew that nothing ever ends, that love, love, knows his way to survive; that every love story is a ghost story.

"Every love story is a ghost story" is my first album. Entirely written, produced, recorded, mastered and mixed by me, in Milan, in my room. It brings together 80s music, shoegaze, drone, and pop music.
